The Ultimate Spiced Hot Dark Chocolate

Description

In "Chocolat," Vianne (Juliette Binoche) finds herself in unfriendly territory when she opens a chocolate shop in a conservative town in rural France. But she eventually wins over the villagers with her recipes, including a sensual and spicy hot chocolate drink.

Ingredients

  • 1 quart almond milk, preferably homemade, or soy milk
  • 6 ounces dark chocolate (at least 70 percent cacao), chopped
  • ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/8 to 1/4 teaspoon ground cardamom, to taste
  • ⅛ teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • ⅛ teaspoon coarse salt

Steps

  1. Heat all ingredients in a small saucepan over medium-low, whisking constantly, until chocolate has melted and mixture is steaming, about 5 minutes. Serve immediately.
